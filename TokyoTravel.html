Tokyo is one of the most important cities in the world and has a long and interesting history.

Tokyo Tower ( Japanese :東京タワー; Tōkyō tāwa ; English : Tokyo Tower ) is a large communications tower in Minato ward, Tokyo , Japan. It is 332.6 metres (1,091 ft) high. It was completed on October 14, 1958. In addition to being a tower for transmitting various television signals such as NHK, TBS , etc., Tokyo Tower is also one of the most important tourist attractions in Tokyo. Over 2.5 million people visit the tower per year . The tower area can be divided into 3 parts. The lowest part is a 4-story building located directly below the tower. Inside, there are museums, shops, restaurants, etc. The remaining 2 parts are observation decks of the tower, located at heights of 150 meters and 250 meters respectively .
