Hokkaido Asahiyama Zoo


Asahiyama Zoo Story: Penguins in the Sky ( Japanese :旭山動物園物語 penguins in the sky ; English : Asahiyama Zoo Story: Penguins in the Sky ) is a 2009 Japanese film.


The story takes place in Asahiyama Zoo, where the new keeper, Yoshida Tsuyoshi (Yasuhi Nakamura), who is not very social, is faced with a financial crisis in the early 1990s, which almost leads to the zoo's closure. However, under the guidance of the manager, Takizawa Kanji (Toshiyuki Nishida ), Yoshida soon finds a way to help the financially troubled zoo, using ideas such as advice from experts in various animal behaviors, and by opening the Asahiyama Night Zoo, the zoo begins to regain its reputation



Main cast

Yasuhi Nakamura as Yoshida Tsuyoshi

Toshiyuki Nishida as Takizawa Kanji
